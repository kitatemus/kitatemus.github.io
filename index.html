I specialize in producing packaging, branding and posters for museums, festivals, bands and more. Most of the day I have two things on my mind, graphic design and music. I find influence in Libraries, Punk Rock music, Art History, Illustration, The Food Network and independently- run silkscreen shops. My process involves completely submerging myself into the project. It is especially important that the client and designer are on the same level of understanding. My execution process often involves photography, not only because of the control and minipulation possible, but I feel this breaks a barrier between designer & audiance. To get a copy of my resume or contact me, email me at danieljjurman@gmail.com
